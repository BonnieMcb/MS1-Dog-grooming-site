Our Team

Sarah Cloudberry

Due to her love of all things canine, Sarah decided to start her own grooming business after 10 years of grooming experience. She is a fully qualified groomer, holding a City and Guilds Level 3 Diploma for Dog Grooming Stylists, as well as Diplomas in First Aid and in Handling Dangerous Dogs. Sarah is the proud owner of Ned, a schnauzer.

Danny Mc Gettigan

Danny has been grooming dogs for around 7 years and is a fully qualified City and Guilds Dog Groomer. Last year he won 2nd prize in the British Dog Grooming Championships. He is the proud owner of his adopted dog Thor.
